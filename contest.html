Cuadra Photography Contest

The Cuadra Photography Contest is an annual event celebrating the beauty of nature through the lens of amateur photographers. Open to individuals aged 18 and above, this contest invites participants to submit their best photographs capturing the essence of variety of themes. Participants can submit up to three entries, each showcasing their creativity, composition, and technical skills. Winners stand a chance to win cash prizes, with the first-place winner also earning the opportunity to showcase their work in a photography exhibition. Join us in this celebration of photography and nature's wonders!

Rules and Regulations

  1. The competition is open to amateur photographers aged 18 and above.
  2. All photographs must adhere to the above themes.
  3. The deadline for submitting entries is September 30, 2024, at 11:59 PM PST.
  4. Photographs must be in JPEG format and minimal editing is allowed.
  5. Participants retain the copyright to their images but grant the competition organizers the right to use submitted images for promotional purposes.
  6. Entries will be judged based on creativity, composition, and technical quality.
  7. Prizes:
    • First place: $500 cash prize and a photography exhibition.
    • Second place: $300 cash prize.
    • Third place: $200 cash prize.
    • 4th and 5th will get consolation Prize of $50
  8. Winners will be notified via email by October 15, 2024.
  9. Participants will be disqualified for submitting plagiarized or inappropriate content.
  10. By entering the competition, participants grant permission for the organizers to publicize winning entries and participants' names.
  11. The organizers are not liable for any lost or damaged entries.
  12. The organizers reserve the right to make changes to the rules if necessary.
  13. Participants must adhere to a code of conduct and submit only original photographs.
  14. There is no entry fee for participating in the competition.
  15. All entries must comply with applicable laws and regulations.
